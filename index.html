Our Story

Happy Belly is the first eco virtual restaurant in Lagos Nigeria, serving on-demand and customizable gourment meals founded by professional chefs. Jennifer Adebisi and Uche Banye to address limited access to nutritious, high quality meals, over dependence on imported ingredients, environmental harm and food wastage.

We have pioneered a sustainable food system, sourcing 100% organic produce locally, adopting eco-friendly practices, and educating our community to minimize environmental impact.

We believe the future of food is sustainable let's shape it together one eco-conscious meal at a time!
